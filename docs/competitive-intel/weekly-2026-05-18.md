# Competitive Intelligence — Week of 2026-05-18

## Summary

No high-priority threats emerged this week. The most strategically notable recent
development is the **collapse of Castor** (filed for liquidation in mid-2025), removing

## 2. Slicer Releases

**No orientation-relevant releases this week** from Cura, PrusaSlicer, Bambu Studio,
OrcaSlicer, Chitubox, Netfabb, or Materialise Magics.

**State of play:**

- **OrcaSlicer** — On v2.3.x; v3.0 reportedly in pre-release (Spring 2026 window).
  Auto-orient feature exists but uses simple heuristics (minimize overhang area, no
  gradient descent or Pareto). No changelog entry targeting orientation this week.
  Watch: if 3.0 ships with improved auto-orient, this is the closest open-source
  competitor to orientIQ's core loop.
  — [Releases](https://github.com/OrcaSlicer/OrcaSlicer/releases)

- **Bambu Studio** — Auto-orient tests 4 discrete rotations (0°, 45°, 90°, 135°),
  not continuous optimization. No May 2026 release notes found touching orientation.
  — [Auto Orientation wiki](https://wiki.bambulab.com/en/software/bambu-studio/auto-orientation)

- **Chitubox** — Redesigned support panel (late 2025) with faster overhang detection
  and "Magic Support" batch mode; no orientation optimizer upgrade this week.
  — [3Dnatives coverage](https://www.3dnatives.com/en/chitubox-reinvents-itself-a-faster-smarter-and-more-connected-slicer-290920254/)

## 3. Startups / Commercial Players

- **Castor — filed for liquidation (2025, confirmed)**
  Israeli startup that analyzed CAD files for AM feasibility (part orientation
  adjacency, make-vs-buy). Filed for court-ordered liquidation citing ~ILS 8.6M
  (~$2.3M) in debt and AM market contraction.
  **Why it matters:** Removes the only startup that competed on "geometry-driven AM
  decision support." The gap in mid-market tooling for orientation + Pareto trade-off
  analysis is now wider. Potential opportunity to absorb displaced Castor users.
  — [3DPrint.com](https://3dprint.com/319042/israeli-3d-printing-software-startup-castor-files-for-liquidation/)

- **Atlas 3D / Sunata — fully absorbed by Siemens (prior acquisition)**
  Sunata's Thermal Circuit Network (TCN) technology for DMLS orientation is now
  inside Siemens NX AM. No standalone product, no new releases this week. OrientIQ
  competes only indirectly (FDM/SLA vs. DMLS, open library vs. enterprise suite).
  — [Siemens acquisition announcement](https://ideacenter.nd.edu/news-events/news/notre-dame-startup-success-siemens-expands-additive-manufacturing-portfolio-with-acquisition-of-atlas-3d/)

- **AMPower / other packing-focused players** — No news found this week.
