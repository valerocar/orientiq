## 2. Slicer Release Notes

### Autodesk Netfabb 2026 — **RELEVANT**

- **What:** Netfabb 2026 shipped with (a) **True Shape packing** — a fully
  rewritten nesting engine designed for serial production, supporting
  multi-platform distribution and part duplication natively; (b) improved
  **support orientation control** relative to the horizontal plane to prevent
  deformation; (c) redesigned UI streamlining access to support generation
  and orientation tools; (d) deeper **3MF** file format support (color,
  texture, material in one file); (e) new machine workspace templates for
  Aconity3D, Farsoon, Formlabs, HP, Stratasys, and others.
- **Why it matters:** True Shape packing is a direct competitive move in
  the build-plate packing space (adjacent to orientation). The support
  orientation control update suggests Netfabb is closing gaps in automated
  support planning — territory where orientIQ could differentiate. Netfabb
  targets industrial/enterprise buyers; our library targets engineers and
  researchers who need a programmable API.
- **Source:** [3D Printing Industry](https://3dprintingindustry.com/news/autodesk-updates-netfabb-with-enhanced-workflow-tools-and-new-3mf-support-in-2026-release-238996/)

### Materialise Magics 30 — **WATCH**

- **What:** Magics 30 is in alpha and targeted for Spring 2026 release.
  New features include nTop implicit-geometry integration, a Lattice Module,
  enhanced Nester Module (auto-placement + protective packaging), and
  support generation workflow improvements. Licensing moves to mandatory
  ID-based system.
- **Why it matters:** nTop integration is significant — it lets Magics handle
  implicit/lattice geometries without STL conversion, a pipeline gap orientIQ
  currently also has (trimesh is STL/OBJ only). Nester improvements reinforce
  Materialise's lead in build-plate packing for SLS/metal. Not a direct threat
  to our core orientation-optimization kernel, but sets the feature bar for
  what enterprise users expect alongside orientation.
- **Source:** [Materialise what's new](https://www.materialise.com/en/industrial/software/magics-data-build-preparation/whats-new) | [Magics 30 Alpha docs](https://help.materialise.com/magics-30-alpha)
